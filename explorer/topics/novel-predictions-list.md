# Topic: Compile the Genuinely Novel Predictions

## Question
What predictions does Synchronism make that are genuinely novel — not rederivations of known physics in new notation? The honest assessment page says "0 unique confirmed predictions." But what are the unique *unconfirmed* ones?

## Context

## Why It Matters

Without a clear answer, the site's value proposition is unclear. This could become a dedicated page or a prominent section on the honest assessment.
