# Topic: Key Claims — Where Can Synchronism Move the Needle?

## Priority: HIGH (dp directive)

## The Question
What are the 3-5 claims where Synchronism genuinely says something new — not restatements of existing physics in different notation, not philosophical reframing, but claims that would advance understanding if confirmed?

## dp's Assessment
Two standout areas, in order:

### 1. The Quantum Reframe (biggest standout)
The Copernican analogy — observer-centric QM is the same category error as Ptolemaic astronomy — is intellectually striking but currently buried in `/observer-problem/`. More importantly, the research repo contains predictions that ACTUALLY DIFFER from standard QM, but they never made it to the site:

- **Shared-environment decoherence protection**: Entangled pairs in the SAME noise environment decohere SLOWER than pairs in independent environments. Standard QM's local decoherence model predicts no such difference. (Sessions #233, #237)
- **Bell violation temporal decay**: Bell violations decay with delay following Γ = γ²(1-c) where c is environmental noise correlation. Standard QM predicts no distance/time dependence. (Session #237)
- **Decoherence rate formula**: Γ = γ²(1-c) — a specific, falsifiable functional form. (Session #233)
- **Entanglement as ONE extended phase pattern**, not two correlated systems — the "one pattern" ontology that goes beyond "shared coherence."

These are the quantum claims that survive the reparametrization test. The 6 protocols currently on `/quantum-predictions/` are all framed as "untested" but none are highlighted as departing from standard QM. That's a massive presentation gap.

### 2. Free Will / Consciousness Equations
- C = f(γ, D, S) is a quantitative consciousness formula — rare in the field
- 8-way convergence at C ≈ 0.50 is compelling (range 0.48-0.52 from independent approaches)
- "Constrained indeterminacy at γ ≈ 1" is a genuine third option (not compatibilism, not libertarian, not hard determinism)
- BUT: the free will page is the weakest in the consciousness cluster — no equation, no link to C formula, admits no distinct predictions
- The Gnosis cross-validation (AI architecture independently finding same threshold through 4 different mathematical frameworks) is untapped and would be the strongest non-cosmology empirical argument

### Also notable but secondary:
- **Dark matter as incomplete decoherence** — a striking reframe (regions where decoherence isn't complete, not a new particle species). Listed on publication-roadmap at 0.88 readiness but invisible in conceptual pages.
- **Born rule grounded in a conserved physical quantity** — no prior derivation does this. The page undersells by crediting Gleason/Zurek/Carroll-Sebens without stating what's actually new.

A new page (or restructured `/why-synchronism/`) that presents 3-5 claims as:
- **The claim** (one sentence)
- **Why it matters** (what changes if true)

Quantum reframe claims should lead because they have the most specific, falsifiable, standard-QM-departing predictions. Consciousness claims follow because the 8-way convergence is genuinely unusual even if untested.
